Chris Woods is a British Animator, currently based in Sydney Australia.

His love for animation is inspired by his fascination of movement, whether that's surfing, running or skateboarding. Whilst he specialises in gaming, he's proficient in all areas of animation, with a curiosity for exploring new programmes and techniques.

He's had the pleasure of working with a variety of studios and clients from London to Sydney, including Larian Studios, Plastic Wax, Airship Interactive, Woolworths Iris, Samsung, Pandora & Starbucks.
